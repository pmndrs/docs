[Buffer-geometry](https://threejs.org/docs/index.html#api/en/core/BufferGeometry) short-cut for Lathe that creates meshes with axial symmetry like vases. The lathe rotates around the Y axis.

```jsx
<Lathe args={[points]}>
  <meshPhongMaterial attach="material" color="#f3f3f3" />
</Lathe>
```
