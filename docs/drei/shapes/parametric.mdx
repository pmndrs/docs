---

[Buffer-geometry](https://threejs.org/docs/index.html#api/en/core/BufferGeometry) short-cut for Parametric that generates geometry representing a parametric surface.

```jsx
<Parametric args={[ParametricGeometries.klein, 25, 25]}>
  <meshPhongMaterial attach="material" color="#f3f3f3" />
</Parametric>
```
